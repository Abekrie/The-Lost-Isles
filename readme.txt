Manual Installation Instructions
1) Download the mod
2) Delete any former versions
3) Extract the .zip to 'Documents\Paradox Interactive\Hearts of Iron IV\mod'
4) Rename the folder from 'The Lost Isles' to 'lost-isles'
5) Move 'lost-isles.mod' out of 'Hearts of Iron IV\mod\lost-isles' and into 'Hearts of Iron IV\mod'
6) Enable the mod in the Launcher mod menu and enjoy!
